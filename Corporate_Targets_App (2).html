📋 Instructions
  1. Tap the ＋ Add Corporate Target tab at the top of the screen.
  2. Enter the company name — the app will warn you if a similar entry already exists.
  3. Fill in the Contact Name and Committee Contact, then click Add Corporate Target.
  4. Once you receive a response from the company, find them in the Corporate List and update their Status (Confirmed / Pending / Not Interested).
  5. If they are sponsoring, also set their Sponsorship Type (Platinum or Gold).
